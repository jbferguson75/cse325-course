We will be utilizing free Microsoft learning content tutorials available online for ASP.NET in our curriculum. These resources offer comprehensive, up-to-date guidance and practical exercises to enhance your understanding and proficiency in ASP.NET development.

Staying up-to-date with the latest technologies and best practices is essential in the fast-paced world of software development. Employers seek candidates who possess attitudes that understand that learning is a lifelong process and often self-motivated. This course will provide you with the tools and resources to help you develop a habit of continuous learning using freely available and proven content.

The course provides a learning environment the provides and organized approach to learning, group work experience, and where you have access to a mentor.
